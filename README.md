# 📊 Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis (Tableau)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is part of the **Data Science & Analytics Internship – Task 3**.
The goal of this analysis is to evaluate marketing campaign performance by identifying **conversion trends, customer behavior, and optimization opportunities**.

The dashboard provides insights into how different customer segments and campaign factors influence conversion outcomes.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* Analyze customer data to understand conversion behavior
* Identify high-performing marketing channels
* Detect drop-offs and optimization opportunities
* Provide actionable business recommendations

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* Tableau (Dashboard & Visualization)
* Microsoft Excel (Data Preparation)

---

## 📁 Dataset

* Bank Marketing Dataset (Campaign-based customer data)
* Includes customer demographics, contact methods, and campaign outcomes

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

* **Total Customers Contacted**
* **Converted Customers**
* **Conversion Rate (%)**

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes:

* Conversion Rate KPI
* Channel-wise Conversion Analysis
* Customer Segment Analysis (Job, Education)
* Time-based Trends (Monthly Performance)
* Campaign Effectiveness Insights
* Interactive Filters (Contact, Job, Month)

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 📌 Overall Performance

* Conversion Rate: **11.70%**
* Total Converted Customers: **5,289**
* Indicates moderate campaign effectiveness with room for improvement

---

### 📞 Channel Performance

* **Cellular** is the best-performing channel (~14–15%)
* **Telephone** performs slightly lower (~13–14%)
* **Unknown channel** performs poorly (~5–6%)

👉 Insight: Properly tracked communication channels significantly improve conversions

---

### 👥 Customer Segment Insights (Job)

* **Students (~30%)** and **Retired (~25%)** show highest conversion
* **Blue-collar & Entrepreneur** segments perform moderately
* **Admin & Management** show lower engagement

👉 Insight: Younger and financially flexible segments are more responsive

---

### 📅 Time-Based Trends (Month)

* Peak conversions in **March–April**
* Drop during **May–July**
* Recovery in **September–October**
* Decline in **December**

👉 Insight: Campaign timing has a strong impact on success

---

### 🎓 Education-Based Insights

* **Tertiary education (~16–17%)** converts highest
* **Primary education (~7%)** converts lowest

👉 Insight: Higher education correlates with higher conversion likelihood

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

1. Focus on **cellular communication channels**
2. Target **students and retired customers**
3. Schedule major campaigns in **March and September**
4. Prioritize **tertiary-educated audiences**
5. Improve tracking for **unknown channels**

---

## 📌 Conclusion

This analysis highlights key factors influencing marketing success, including **channel effectiveness, customer segmentation, and campaign timing**.
By optimizing these areas, businesses can significantly improve conversion rates and overall performance.
